Energy Percentile Rank

Indicates how a material's linker energy ranks compared to others in a dataset. A rank of 10% means it's in the top 10% of materials from the QMOF, CoRE MOF 2019, and ToBaCCo databases.

RMSD Percentile Rank

Similar to the energy rank, but for the Root Mean Square Deviation (RMSD) of the linker.

Energy

The energy difference between the linker in the MOF structure and its free state.

RMSD

The difference in RMSD between the linker in the MOF and its free state.

Smiles

The SMILES code represents the structure of the linker.

Single Point Energy

The energy of the linker in the MOF conformation.

Optimized Energy

The energy of the linker after being optimized in its free state.

Status

Indicates whether the optimization process was successful.
